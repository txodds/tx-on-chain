## General

**What is TxLINE?** TxLINE is a sports data API delivering fixtures, odds, scores, and settlement data. It publishes Merkle roots on Solana so supported proof-backed records can be checked against the on-chain commitment.

**Ready to build?** Start with the [Quickstart Guide](https://txline.txodds.com/documentation/quickstart).

**Who is TxLINE designed for?** Developers, sportsbooks, betting exchanges, prediction markets, analytics platforms, and blockchain-based betting products.

**What makes TxLINE different from other sports data APIs?** TxLINE anchors Merkle roots on Solana and provides validation flows for supported fixture, score, and odds records. Callers can verify a returned proof against the matching on-chain commitment instead of trusting the response alone.

**Where can I get help?** Documentation and an API reference are available at [txline.txodds.com](https://txline.txodds.com). Enterprise customers can access dedicated support with their Account Manager. Alternatively, email [hello@txodds.com](mailto:hello@txodds.com).

## Getting Started

**How do I get started?** Create a Solana wallet, subscribe to a tier, activate an API token, and make your first request. The free tier requires no TxL payment, but the on-chain transaction still requires SOL for fees and possible account rent.

**What do I need before I start?** A Solana wallet is required to activate any subscription, including the free tier. For paid tiers, you'll also need USDT on the Solana network to purchase TxL tokens.

**Do I need a crypto wallet or cryptocurrency to use TxLINE?**

Crypto wallet: Yes. A Solana wallet is required for every tier, including the free tier, because subscription state is recorded on-chain. Cryptocurrency: Free subscriptions require SOL for transaction fees and possible account rent, but no TxL payment. Paid subscriptions require TxL tokens purchased using USDT on Solana. Know Your Customer (KYC) verification may apply.

**How do I purchase TxL tokens?** Fund your Solana wallet with USDT and request a purchase quote. The backend returns a serialized transaction. The current Quickstart signs it but does not implement local transaction verification, so validate its programs, instructions, writable and signing accounts, fee payer, mint, recipients, amount, blockhash assumptions, and unrelated instructions before using the flow with funds.

**Is KYC required?** KYC verification may be required for TxL token purchases depending on compliance requirements.

## Free Tier & World Cup

**Is there a free tier, and what does it include?** Yes. World Cup and International Friendlies data is available with no TxL payment or credit card. Mainnet documents free 60-second and real-time service levels. The current devnet pricing row exposes free service level `1` with `samplingIntervalSec = 0`. See [Subscription Tiers](https://txline.txodds.com/documentation/subscription-tiers).

**What does the World Cup feed include?** The World Cup feed includes:

- Live scores and score updates
- Match events: goals, cards, substitutions, and more
- Match status and period changes
- Fixtures and scheduling data
- Odds data (via the StablePrice feed)
- Historical score replay for fixtures within the documented retention window

**Are there any other live event types planned for the World Cup feed?** Use the current [TxLINE documentation](https://txline.txodds.com) for supported event types, or contact [hello@txodds.com](mailto:hello@txodds.com).

**Can I use the free tier for commercial projects?** Yes, including commercial projects. Mainnet service level `12` already provides real-time World Cup and International Friendlies access at no TxL cost. Paid tiers expand league coverage and bundle choices.

**Is there a rate limit on the free tier?** The on-chain pricing matrix does not define HTTP rate limits, and the current public repository does not establish a no-limit guarantee. Treat `429` as a rate-limit response, back off, and confirm current API policy with TxODDS.

**Do I need to renew my free subscription?** Subscriptions use positive 4-week multiples. When access expires, re-subscribe. The current free row has zero TxL price, but it can change and the transaction still requires SOL for fees and possible account rent.

## Data & Sports Coverage

**What sports and competitions are available?** Soccer includes the FIFA World Cup and International Friendlies on the free tier, with additional league bundles in paid plans. The score documentation also covers US college football and basketball encodings. Check the current schedule and subscription matrix for live coverage.

**What data can I access?** Depending on your subscription: fixtures, live scores, match events, odds, historical data, real-time updates, and on-chain settlement information.

**How is data delivered?** TxLINE uses a REST API and Server-Sent Events (SSE). The historical scores endpoint covers fixtures whose start times are between two weeks and six hours in the past.

**What is the difference between 60-second delayed and real-time data?**

60-second delayed: Data is delayed by 60 seconds for latency-tolerant applications.

Real-time: Data is delivered without the 60-second tier delay. A real-time tier can be free or paid depending on its league bundle; mainnet service level `12` is free.

**What is on-chain verification and why does it matter?**

Every data update is cryptographically hashed and its Merkle root is published to Solana. You can use the matching proof and on-chain root to verify that a record belongs to a published batch. Verification depends on selecting the correct record, timestamp, proof bytes, and program account.

## Subscriptions & Pricing

**How do subscriptions work? Can I choose specific leagues?**

The `durationWeeks` argument is an unsigned 8-bit value and must be a positive multiple of four. Current devnet simulations accepted `4`, `52`, `56`, and `252`, and rejected `0`, `2`, `5`, and `255`; `252` is the largest representable multiple of four. Verify the selected network's current pricing account before submitting. Custom league subscriptions can select competitions instead of a standard bundle where the service level supports them.

Review current bundles in [Subscription Tiers](https://txline.txodds.com/documentation/subscription-tiers).

**Can I upgrade from the free tier to a paid tier?** You can submit a paid subscription for a broader league bundle. Confirm the on-chain transaction and activate its API token before relying on the new entitlement.

**What happens if I don't renew?** API access expires at the end of the subscription period. Re-subscribe at any time to restore access.
